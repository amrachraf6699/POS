# Phase 09 — Subscriptions and Stripe

Implement the Subscriptions module with trial, Starter/Business plans, usage limits, Stripe subscription boundaries, signed webhooks, grace periods, and billing audit history.

## Dependencies

Phase 01 and operational usage data from Phases 02–08.

## Limits

Starter: 1 branch, 3 users, 1 register, 1,000 products, 2,000 monthly sales. Business: 5 branches, 20 users, 10 registers, 20,000 products, 25,000 monthly sales. Trial uses Business limits for a configurable period.
